Contact Us

Do you have questions about the CAT project or perhaps you have information about a CAT-related topic? We welcome both questions and tips.

Examples of the kinds of information we might be interested in: information about trouble caused by wild boar in particular areas of Europe; debates about migratory birds; problems with parakeets in European cities; outbreaks of African Swine Fever and responses; debates about wolves or other controversial wild animals; mass extinctions; rewilding projects; debates about intensive or industrial animal farming; outbreaks of bird flu; discussions about invasive species of any kind; debates about hunting. These are just a few examples: any current story about some kind of conflict or discussion caused by people-animal relations would be welcome. Please use the form below to get in touch.
